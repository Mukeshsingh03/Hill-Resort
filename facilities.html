    Tarikhet Village

    Tarikhet is small, yet popular hill station situated at distance of 8 km from the town of Ranikhet. This village is a famous as a momentous site during the India’s struggle for independence. It’s most popular attraction is the ‘Gandhi Kutiya’, which is told to be the place where Mahatma Gandhi rested for a long time during the freedom struggle.

    Binsar Mahadev Temple

    Binsar is best known for its scenic natural beauty, especially for the views of snowclad mountains from Zero Point. It's an ideal destination for nature-lovers who are looking for a refuge from the hustle and bustle of city life. Besides, this place is also famous for its rich flora and fauna

    Sparrow Bird

    Sparrows have beautiful voices and their chirping and singing can be heard all over. Other unique characteristics are their smooth, round heads and rounded wings. Males have reddish feathers on their backs and females are brown and striped.
