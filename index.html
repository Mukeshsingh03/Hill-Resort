Ranikhet hill resort
Ranikhet, literally meaning 'Queen's Meadow', is a popular hill station in the Almora district of Uttarakhand . Surrounded by hills, this is a cantonment town was frequented by the British during their rule, to escape the summer heat. Located at an elevation of 1,869 m above sea level , tourists can enjoy the pleasant weather and the pictures views the place has to offer.

Ranikhet hill resort is one of the top luxury resort in the mystic and mesmerizing land of Ranikhet. Sheltered on a hill-top and surrounded with majestic Himalayan ranges, it is a place with utter quiescent vibes where you can spend time of solitude and peace.
